<p><p>The Electronically Controlled Limited-Slip Differential (ECLSD) market includes three key types: Electronic Hydraulic, Electromagnetic, and Electric Mechanical. Electronic Hydraulic systems utilize hydraulic pressure controlled electronically to manage torque distribution between wheels, enhancing traction. Electromagnetic systems use magnetic fields to adjust the locking mechanism, providing quick response to changing conditions. Electric Mechanical differentials employ electric motors to manage torque transfer, offering precise control. Each type aims to improve vehicle stability, traction, and performance under various driving conditions.</p></p>
