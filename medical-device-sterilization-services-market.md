<p><p>The Medical Device Sterilization Services market encompasses various methods to eliminate pathogens from medical devices. Ethylene oxide (ETO) sterilization is widely used, particularly for heat-sensitive items, due to its effectiveness at low temperatures. Steam sterilization, or autoclaving, utilizes high-pressure steam for durable materials. Electron beam sterilization employs high-energy electrons for rapid disinfection, making it suitable for certain plastics and electronics. Other methods include gamma irradiation and hydrogen peroxide vapor, each offering unique advantages based on device materials and intended use.</p></p>
