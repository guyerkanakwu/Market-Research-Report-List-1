<p><strong>The Resistant Potato Starch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RS2</li><li>RS3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistant potato starch market is categorized into two main types: RS2 and RS3. RS2 is the raw, unmodified starch found in high-amylose varieties of potatoes, providing health benefits like improved gut health and blood sugar regulation. RS3, on the other hand, is retrograded starch formed when cooked and cooled potatoes re-assimilate, enhancing its resistance to digestion. Both types serve as functional ingredients in food products, promoting dietary fiber intake and supporting health-conscious consumer trends.</p></p>
