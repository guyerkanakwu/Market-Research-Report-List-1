<p><p>The lazy eye market comprises three primary types: **Strabismic Amblyopia**, characterized by misalignment of the eyes leading to dual vision, **Refractive Amblyopia**, caused by uncorrected refractive errors like nearsightedness or farsightedness, and **Deprivation Amblyopia**, which results from an obstruction in visual input, such as cataracts. Each type presents unique challenges and treatment pathways, driving demand for specific therapies and interventions aimed at improving visual function and overall eye health in affected individuals.</p></p>
