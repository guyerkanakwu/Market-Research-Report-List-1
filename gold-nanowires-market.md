<p><strong>The Gold Nanowires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Phase</li><li>Water/Amine Phase</li><li>Water Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gold nanowires market can be segmented into three primary types based on the synthesis phase: oil phase, water/amine phase, and water phase. Oil phase synthesis typically leads to better control over the particle size and morphology, enhancing conductivity. In contrast, water/amine phase processes utilize amine surfactants to stabilize nanowires in aqueous environments, making them suitable for biocompatible applications. Water phase synthesis emphasizes environmentally friendly methods, but may compromise particle uniformity, often requiring post-synthesis modifications for desired properties.</p></p>
