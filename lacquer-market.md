<p><strong>The Lacquer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Lacquer</li><li>Water-based Lacquer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lacquer market consists of two primary types: solvent-based lacquer and water-based lacquer. Solvent-based lacquers use organic solvents as a carrier, providing a durable finish and excellent adhesion, ideal for wood applications but emitting volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based lacquers utilize water as their primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions, easier cleanup, and reduced odor, making them increasingly popular for indoor applications and environmentally conscious consumers. Both options serve distinct needs in the finishing industry.</p></p>
