<p><p>In the V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) charging station market, there are two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional V2G. Unidirectional V2G refers to systems where electric vehicles (EVs) only draw power from the grid, primarily for charging without sending energy back. In contrast, bidirectional V2G allows for two-way energy flow, enabling EVs to discharge stored energy back to the grid. This capability benefits grid stability and energy management while offering EV owners potential financial incentives or savings.</p></p>
