<p><p>The under-eye concealer market comprises two main types: liquid and paste formulations. Liquid concealers are lightweight and provide buildable coverage, ideal for a natural finish. They often contain hydrating ingredients, making them suitable for delicate skin. In contrast, paste concealers are thicker and offer a more opaque coverage, effectively hiding dark circles and blemishes. Paste formulas are typically applied with a brush or fingertip, allowing for precise application, making them popular for those seeking long-lasting, intense coverage.</p></p>
