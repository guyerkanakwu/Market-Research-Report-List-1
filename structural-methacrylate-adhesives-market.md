<p><p>Structural methacrylate adhesives are categorized based on their mixing ratios, which significantly impact their performance characteristics. The 1:1 ratio is ideal for general applications, providing balanced properties for bonding. The 4:1 ratio offers a faster cure time and enhanced flexibility, suitable for high-stress environments. The 10:1 ratio, while slower to cure, delivers superior strength and durability, making it appropriate for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs, ensuring versatility in bonding solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
