<p><p>The campground booking system market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. This model typically offers scalability and easier updates. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on the campground's servers, providing complete control over data and operations but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance.
