The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) market utilizes radio waves to communicate between key fobs and vehicle receivers, enabling seamless unlocking and starting. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology offers a low-power alternative, allowing smartphones or devices to connect with the vehicle through secure Bluetooth signals. Both technologies enhance user experience through convenience and security, driving growth in the automotive industry as modern vehicles adopt advanced entry solutions.</p></p>
