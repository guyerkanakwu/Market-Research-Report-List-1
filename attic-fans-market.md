<p><strong>The Attic Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rooftop-mounted</li><li>Ceiling-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Attic fans are typically categorized into two primary market types: rooftop-mounted and ceiling-mounted. Rooftop-mounted fans are installed on the roof, effectively exhausting hot air directly from the attic space, enhancing ventilation and cooling efficiency. Ceiling-mounted fans, on the other hand, are positioned within the attic, drawing in cooler air from outside while expelling hot air. Both types help regulate attic temperatures, improve energy efficiency, and extend roof lifespan by preventing heat buildup, catering to diverse residential and commercial needs.</p></p>
