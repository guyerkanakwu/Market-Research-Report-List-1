<p><p>The injection flu shot market is primarily categorized into two types: trivalent and quadrivalent influenza vaccines. Trivalent vaccines target three strains of the influenza virus, typically two A strains and one B strain, offering protection against the most common circulating viruses. In contrast, quadrivalent vaccines include an additional B strain, providing broader protection against four strains. This expansion allows for improved efficacy in preventing influenza, addressing the evolving nature of the virus and increasing public health safety.</p></p>
