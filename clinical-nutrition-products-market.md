<p><strong>The Clinical Nutrition Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parenteral Nutrition</li><li>Enteral Nutrition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clinical nutrition products market is primarily categorized into parenteral and enteral nutrition. Parenteral nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly into the bloodstream through an intravenous route, catering to patients who cannot consume food orally or digest it properly. Enteral nutrition, on the other hand, provides essential nutrients via the gastrointestinal tract, typically through feeding tubes for patients with functioning digestive systems but unable to eat normally. Both types play a crucial role in managing nutritional needs in clinical settings.</p></p>
