<p><strong>The Digital Pathology Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Telepathology</li><li>Dynamic Telepathology</li><li>Hybrid Telepathology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Pathology Systems encompass various market types, including Static Telepathology, Dynamic Telepathology, and Hybrid Telepathology. Static Telepathology involves the remote sharing of pre-captured images for diagnosis, often used for educational purposes. Dynamic Telepathology allows real-time image transmission and consultation, facilitating live interactions between pathologists. Hybrid Telepathology combines both static and dynamic methods, offering flexibility and versatility in digital diagnostics. Together, these types enhance collaboration, efficiency, and accuracy in pathological evaluations across diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
