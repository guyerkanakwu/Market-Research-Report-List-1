<p><ul><li>Equal-Leg-Angle</li><li>Unequal-Leg-Angle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The L-Section steel market is categorized primarily into two types: Equal-Leg Angle and Unequal-Leg Angle. Equal-Leg Angle features legs of the same length, providing balanced structural support, often used in frameworks and supports. Conversely, Unequal-Leg Angle has legs of different lengths, offering versatility in complex applications where load distribution varies. Both types are vital in construction and manufacturing, catering to diverse engineering needs, enhancing stability, and ensuring load-bearing efficiency in various structural designs.</p></p>
