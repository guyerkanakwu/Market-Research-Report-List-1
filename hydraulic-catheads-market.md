<p><p>The hydraulic catheads market is primarily segmented into two types: pneumatic and electrical. In contrast, electrical catheads leverage electric power for operation, providing consistent performance and reduced maintenance requirements. Both types are crucial in drilling operations, facilitating the handling of heavy drill pipes and improving operational efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
