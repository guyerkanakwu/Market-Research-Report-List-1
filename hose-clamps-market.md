<p><ul><li>Screw/Band (Worm Gear) Clamps</li><li>Spring Clamps</li><li>Wire Clamps</li><li>Ear Clamps</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hose clamps market encompasses various types designed for securing hoses in automotive and industrial applications. Screw/band (worm gear) clamps provide adjustable tension through a screw mechanism, ensuring a tight fit. Spring clamps use a spring for automatic adjustment based on hose size. Wire clamps consist of a simple wire loop, offering basic security. Ear clamps are metal bands with "ears" that lock in place, preventing slippage. Additionally, other types may include specialty clamps for unique applications, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
