<p><p>The cellular concrete market comprises various types, including gravel, sand, fly ash, and foaming agents. Gravel is used for enhancing structural strength, while sand helps in achieving desired texture and workability. Fly ash, a byproduct of coal combustion, enhances durability and reduces environmental impact. Foaming agents create air bubbles, resulting in lightweight and insulating properties. Together, these components contribute to the versatility and sustainability of cellular concrete in construction, offering improved performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
