<p><p>The ferrite core inductor market is segmented into several types based on the composition of the ferrite materials. Manganese-Zinc (Mn-Zn) ferrite inductors are widely used for lower frequency applications due to their high permeability and saturation magnetization. Nickel-Zinc (Ni-Zn) ferrite inductors are preferred for higher frequency applications, offering lower core losses. Magnesium-Zinc (Mg-Zn) ferrites provide a balance between properties, serving niche applications. The "Other" category encompasses various specialized ferrite materials that cater to specific requirements in electronics.</p></p>
