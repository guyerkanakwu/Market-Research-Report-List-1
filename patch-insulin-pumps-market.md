<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop</li><li>Open-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patch insulin pump market is categorized into two main types: closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop pumps autonomously adjust insulin delivery based on real-time glucose monitoring, significantly improving blood sugar control. They integrate features like continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) and automated dosing. In contrast, open-loop pumps require manual input from users to determine insulin dosages, offering flexibility but less automation. Both systems aim to enhance diabetes management and provide tailored solutions to individuals' specific needs.</p></p>
