<p><p>The microelectronic automatic wire bonding systems market includes semi-automatic and fully automatic bonding systems. In contrast, fully automatic bonding systems operate independently, utilizing advanced robotics and software to perform high-speed bonding with minimal human oversight, improving consistency and throughput.
