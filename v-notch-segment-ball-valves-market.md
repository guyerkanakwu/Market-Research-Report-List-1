<p><p>V-Notch segment ball valves are categorized into two main market types: flanged and flangeless. Flanged V-Notch valves feature bolted connections that allow for easy installation and maintenance in piping systems, providing a secure fit. Conversely, flangeless V-Notch valves utilize a design that enables direct integration into pipelines without flanges, offering a compact solution that reduces weight and space requirements. Both types are designed for precise flow control in various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
