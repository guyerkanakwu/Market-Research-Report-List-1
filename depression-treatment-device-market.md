<p><p>The Depression Treatment Device Market encompasses various technologies. Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS) delivers a constant low current to modulate brain activity. Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation (tACS) uses alternating currents to influence neuronal oscillations. Transcranial Pulsed Current Stimulation (tPCS) applies short bursts of current for targeted stimulation, while Transcranial Random Noise Stimulation (tRNS) introduces random noise currents to enhance brain function. Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT) is a well-established method involving electrical stimulation to induce seizures for therapeutic effects. Other devices include innovative therapies still in development.</p></p>
