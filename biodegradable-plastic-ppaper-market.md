<p><p>The biodegradable plastic paper market comprises two main types based on production techniques: dry pressing and wet pressing. Dry pressing involves compressing a dry mixture of biodegradable materials using heat and pressure, resulting in a strong, lightweight product. In contrast, wet pressing uses a slurry of fibers combined with water to create a paper-like sheet, offering enhanced flexibility and texture. Both methods cater to diverse applications, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact in packaging and disposable products.</p></p>
