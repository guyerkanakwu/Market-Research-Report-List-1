<p><p>Ballistic separators are essential in waste management and recycling, efficiently sorting materials based on their physical properties. The single-stage ballistic separator processes bulk materials in one pass, typically separating heavier and lighter fractions, making it suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, the double-stage ballistic separator consists of two layers of separation, enhancing efficiency by allowing for more refined sorting and higher recovery rates of valuable materials. Both types are vital for improving recycling operations and reducing landfill waste.</p></p>
