<p><ul><li>Bone Graft</li><li>Bone Cement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone repair materials market primarily includes bone grafts and bone cements. Bone grafts, made from autogenous, allogeneic, or synthetic materials, promote bone healing and regeneration by providing support and scaffolding for new bone growth. Bone cements are biocompatible substances, often based on polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA), used to fix implants, fill voids, and stabilize bone structures. Both products are essential in orthopedic and dental surgeries, facilitating effective healing and enhancing patient recovery outcomes.</p></p>
