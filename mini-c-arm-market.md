<p><p>The Mini C-Arm market is primarily divided into two types: Mini C-Arm with Pulsed Fluoroscopy and Mini C-Arm with Continuous Fluoroscopy. Mini C-Arms with Pulsed Fluoroscopy use brief bursts of X-ray exposure, reducing radiation dose while providing high-quality imaging for orthopedic and surgical procedures. In contrast, those with Continuous Fluoroscopy deliver a continuous stream of X-rays, enabling real-time visualization during procedures. Each type serves specific medical needs, impacting their adoption across various clinical settings.</p></p>
